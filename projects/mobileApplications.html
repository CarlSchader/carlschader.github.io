GridSweep
Description

GridSweep is a turn based strategy game for iOS where the user must defeat procedurally generated enemies on a board that's falling apart as the turns elapse. The app works on all iOS devices. This is my project that I am currently working on alongside this website. The project is currently not a viable product quite yet and is not on the app store. If you want to get your hands on current send me an email at my email.
